Nobel prize in economics to Narendra Modi for his thesis on 'The theory of achche din'

Mr. Modi perfected his theory within the confines of his state office, doubling and tripling every statistic that came to mind.
The theory was so baffling that even Dr. Manmohan Singh, the then (part) Prime Minister did not understand it completely,
even though he was an economist himself. It was only after Mr. Steven Spielberg, an acclaimed Hollywood director heard of
this theory through Mr. Anil Ambani, an Indian business tycoon and Mr. Modi fan, promised that he would use the theory on his
new science fiction movie; the world noticed.
